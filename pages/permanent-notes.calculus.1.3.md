### Questions
	- [[Sep 5th, 2021]]
		- #### Find each limit
			- a) $$\lim_{x\to3}(2x-5)$$
				-
				  $$2(3) - 5 = 1$$
			- b) $$\lim_{x\to0}\sqrt{3x + 81}$$
				-
				  $$\sqrt{3(0) + 81} = 9$$
			- c) $$\lim_{x\to1}\frac{x^2-1}{x-1}$$
				-
				  $$\lim_{x\to1}\frac{x^2-1}{x-1} = \frac{(1)^2-1}{1-1}$$
				  $$undefined$$
				-
				  $$\lim_{x\to1}\frac{x^2-1}{x-1} = \lim_{x\to1}\frac{(x+1)  \cancel{(x-1)}}{\cancel{x-1}}$$
				  $$2$$
